## How I work

**1. I build before I write.** Every tutorial is validated on a real machine or cluster. That's why my first-submission approval rate is 95%+.

**2. I write for the reader's job, not the platform's SEO.** I figure out what decision or task the reader has in front of them, then remove anything that doesn't serve it.

**3. I instrument for outcomes.** When possible, I track reads, time-on-page, and (when clients share) downstream signals like support-ticket deflection or time-to-first-successful-call.
